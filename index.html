    Our Story ✨

    Dearest Kindred Spirit,

    Snehkriti was born from endless cups of chai, ink-stained fingers, and a love for stories stitched into fabric.

    Inspired by my grandmother's wooden blocks and a belief that clothes can carry memories — each piece is handcrafted with intention, love, and a touch of poetry.

    Thank you for wearing our story close to your skin 🌸

    With love & gratitude,
